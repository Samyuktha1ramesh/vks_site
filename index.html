Exploration & Blasting

We possess a fleet of 45 DTH Drills, 25 Crawler mounted Drills and experienced crew to carry out operations for Exploration & Blasting holes.

Crawler mounted Drills

Used in mines for blast hole drilling. These drills specialise in vertical/angle and toe holes

At rocky places, crawler mounted drillers are preferred as they have a perfect balance even though the ground level is not in level

DTH Drills

The simple, intuitive controls of DTH drill equipment allow the operator to fine tune the system manually to meet unusual conditions in underwater blasting.

Basic settings can be made rapidly and easily because they are practically located on the control block.
